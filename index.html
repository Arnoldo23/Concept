Introducing Incredible Youth International

Incredible Youth International was born out of a call to see the world become a better place through leaders who demonstrate humility and giving. We believe that the quality of a country resides in the quality of its youth. That is why we invest in them to become responsible citizens. Incredible Youth International promotes the values of transparency, accountability, and respect for human rights, social justice, economic excellence, and integrity.

Our Mission & Values

Vision

To nurture and help the youth realize their potential by creating opportunities that will contribute towards achieving their enterprises and sustainable Development Goals in Uganda, Africa and globally.

Learn More

Mission

To empower young individuals and their communities to be effective agents of change and establish a true network of young entrepreneurs, leaders and innovators.

Learn More

Social Justice

To ensure a fair balance in the distribution of wealth, opportunities, and privileges within a society where individuals's rights are recognized and protected.

Learn More

Excellence & Integrity

To ensure consistency in good character, attitude, quality and standards amongst the youth while showing a consistent and uncompromising adherence to strong moral and ethical principles and values.

Learn More

Transparency & Accountability

To ensure the availability of accurate information about various issues and the commitments and performance of actors whilst holding institutions and actors accountable for their commitments and performance.

Learn More

Economic Excellence

To ensure allocation of scarce resources for production, distribution, and consumption, both individually and collectively through the various technical workshops in conjuction with partner organizations.
